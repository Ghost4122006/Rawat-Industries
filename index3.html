Online Ordering

We offer an online ordering system on your website so customers can place their orders and have them delivered to their homes or offices. This saves customers time and effort by allowing for quick and easy ordering. Customers can also track the status of their orders in real-time.

Delivery Service

You can offer a delivery service for customers who are unable to make in-store purchases or who prefer to have their orders brought to them. Delivery service can also be helpful for customers who are in a hurry or who simply do not have time to come to the store themselves.

Group orders

You can offer exclusive discounts or promotions for customers who place large group orders, such as schools, offices, or events. These discounts and promotions can encourage customers to place larger orders, which can be more profitable for your business.
